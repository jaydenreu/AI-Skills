# Explore The Terrain

Map uncertainty without forcing an early plan. Separate facts, interpretations, options, assumptions, and preferences; mark confidence and use the best available evidence.

## Process

1. Frame the question and inventory knowns, assumptions, materials, and constraints.
2. Inspect or research enough evidence to avoid pure speculation.
3. Compare promising paths, tradeoffs, dead ends, and unresolved questions.
4. Design the cheapest probes that could change the decision.
5. Save only reusable findings or experiment records; keep noise as scratch.
6. Recommend the next probe, decision, brief, or step.
