# Carry The Step

Complete one step only. Treat its brief, decisions, acceptance criteria, non-goals, materials, and source of truth as authoritative.

1. Inspect current state and state the outcome, scope, evidence, and touched surfaces.
2. Make the smallest useful end-to-end change or action.
3. Check it with the strongest practical evidence, refine while focused, and verify every criterion.
4. Record the durable result and its evidence; commit, log, or publish only when the workspace and user authorization call for it.

Evidence may be a test, build, reconciliation, demo, draft, recording, score, review, checklist, or observed result—use the strongest fit, not the longest list. If blocked, report the blocker, attempted evidence, and smallest next action; never imply an unrun check passed.

Finish with the delivered outcome, evidence and result, criteria coverage, durable-record status and location, and true follow-up only.
