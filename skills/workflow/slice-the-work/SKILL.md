# Slice The Work

Create the fewest small end-to-end slices that prove the brief. Slice by outcome, not layer, team, tool, or activity; avoid setup-only tickets unless they unblock several slices.

## Process

1. Extract decisions, outcomes, constraints, non-goals, and evidence from the brief.
2. Draft dependency-ordered slices. Each must produce a visible or verifiable result and preserve covered decision IDs.
3. Label each `AFK` (agent-ready) or `HITL` (human decision/review required).
4. Check coverage and obtain granularity approval unless told to proceed.
5. Invoke `$model-route Recommend` once for the accepted slice set; attach the returned compact route to non-material T0–T1 issues and full route to T2–T5 or material issues.
6. Publish to the agreed tracker.

```md
# <slice>
Type: AFK | HITL
Parent: <brief>
Blocked by: <refs or none>

## Outcome
## Covers
- DEC-001
## End-to-end change
## Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Outcome and main failure path are verified
- [ ] Required control or review is covered
- [ ] Evidence proves the slice
## Out of scope
## Execution route
<generated by $model-route>
```

Finish when every brief decision is covered, dependencies are explicit, and every published issue is routed. Return issue refs, order, AFK/HITL state, and routing status.
